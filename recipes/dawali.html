Dawali Recipe

Everyone knows how delicious a well done dawali really is. I will outline here how to make a lovely dawali recipes and it'll come out perfect!

Ingredients

  • Waraq 'inab
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Spices
  • Tomato sauce

And that's it for the ingredients! Now for the steps to create one!

Steps

  1. Mix the meat and rice together
  2. Add salt and pepper to the mixture
  3. Add spices to mixture
  4. Roll the waraq wtih the meat and rice
  5. Cook it and das it!

Happy eating :)
